Who We Are

Our website idea is about tourism in Egypt and exploring its tourist attractions. We are a team of five people, each with a specific role based on their expertise. We have many features, such as the "Company" section, which is a part of the website dedicated to a company that books tickets for tours to Egyptian tourist sites. Tickets can be booked online, which reduces pressure on ticket counters at tourist attractions. Tourists simply show their ticket on their phone at the entrance and enter immediately. Another feature is VR (Virtual Reality), which is a paid feature. This makes it easier for tourists who find it difficult to travel or don't want to go out because of the heat. They can wear VR glasses and experience being at the location virtually, but through 3D glasses. We also have a feature for volunteers assisting people with disabilities. This is for people with special needs who want to visit tourist attractions in Egypt but are unable to do so independently. We provide them with a volunteer to assist them on their trip. However, before assigning a volunteer, we review their CV to ensure they are suitable to care for someone. Another feature is the "Workshop Place," where we dress tourists in traditional Egyptian clothing, similar to Nubian attire, and let them try their hand at Egyptian crafts, such as pottery making. In the future, we will create a page for "hidden gems" in Egypt, showcasing beautiful places that are not well-known. We will also create an interactive game for children, similar to a puzzle. When the child completes the puzzle, a tourist attraction in Egypt will appear with information about it in a cartoon format. We will also add a voice assistant for visually impaired people and create a completely offline program for tourism in Egypt. We are "Team ExoToura," and thank you.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make tourism in Egypt accessible to everyone, regardless of their ability to travel or their financial situation. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the beauty of Egypt's tourist attractions, and we are committed to making this possible for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every tourist, regardless of their financial means or physical abilities, can enjoy the beauty of Egypt's tourist attractions and not miss out on anything wonderful that Egypt has to offer.
